Exercise Your Rights (DPDP Act)
📖

Right to Information (S.11)

Request a summary of all personal data held about you and the processing activities performed.

✏️

Right to Correction (S.12)

Request correction of inaccurate personal data or completion of incomplete data.

🗑️

Right to Erasure (S.12)

Request deletion of personal data no longer necessary for the purpose it was collected.

📩

Grievance Redressal (S.13)

File a complaint with the DPO. Escalate to DPBI if unresolved within 30 days.

👤

Right to Nominate (S.14)

Nominate a person to exercise your rights in case of death or incapacity.

📤

Data Portability

Export your personal data in a machine-readable format (CSV/JSON).

File a Grievance

Under DPDP Act Section 13, you may file a complaint with the Data Protection Officer. If not resolved within 30 days, escalate to the Data Protection Board of India (DPBI).
