Your Rights under DPDP Act

📖 Right to Information (S.11)

Access a summary of your personal data and how it is processed.

✏️ Right to Correction & Erasure (S.12)

Request correction of inaccurate data or erasure of unnecessary data.

📩 Right to Grievance Redressal (S.13)

File a complaint with the DPO. Escalate to DPBI if unresolved within 30 days.

👤 Right to Nominate (S.14)

Nominate a person to exercise your rights in case of death or incapacity.
